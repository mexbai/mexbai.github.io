3. Cookies

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the secure use of the services provided through our platform. This Privacy Declaration applies to the use of DeepL API for translation and OpenAI API for text generation as part of our platform.

Collection of Personal Information

We collect information that you provide to our chatbot, including text and other data that is used to train our model. This information is not shared with third parties, except for the purpose of using the DeepL and OpenAI APIs for translation and text generation.

Use of Personal Information

The personal information processed through the APIs is used solely for the purpose of providing the translation or text generation services requested by you. We do not save personal information such as phone numbers. The information you provide to our chatbot is used to train our model and improve the services we offer.

Data Retention

The personal information processed through the APIs is retained only for as long as is necessary to provide the requested services. After this, the information is deleted in accordance with our data retention policies. The information you provide to our chatbot is retained for the purpose of training our model and improving our services.

Compliance with EU Data Protection Law

We are committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other EU data protection laws. This means that we take appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the protection of personal information processed through the APIs.

Your Rights

As a data subject under EU data protection laws, you have certain rights with respect to your personal information, including the right to access, rectify, and erase your information. If you have any concerns or questions about the processing of your personal information, please contact us.
